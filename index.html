What is NapaServe?

A trusted advisor
for every decision

NapaServe is a suite of specialized AI agents, each designed to assist a specific role in Napa County District 1. Every agent shares the same core framework — well-being, good jobs, environmental health, community cohesion, and innovation.

Think of it as having a world-class policy advisor, environmental scientist, economic development expert, and innovation incubator available to every person doing important work in the community — anytime, for free.

The Framework

Four pillars.
One community.

01
People
Well-Being & Community Cohesion
Champion young families, protect older residents, and foster belonging across all communities in District 1.
"Does this strengthen the fabric of our community? Who does this leave out?"
02
Place
Environmental Realism & Resilience
Protect water, prepare for wildfire, and ensure healthy land and air — now and for future generations.
"Does this protect the natural systems we depend on? Are we being honest about the risks?"
03
Prosperity
Good Jobs & Economic Diversity
Living wages, local hiring, career pathways. Diversify beyond wine and tourism to build lasting resilience.
"How many well-paying jobs does this create for people who actually live here?"
04
Innovation
Incubate, Accelerate, Realize
Scout emerging ideas, evaluate feasibility, and accelerate bold solutions from concept to community impact.
"Is there a better way? Who is solving this elsewhere? How do we build it here?"
