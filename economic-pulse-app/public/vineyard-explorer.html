About this view

Each shape is a single vineyard field as delineated by the state's satellite-and-imagery crop survey. This is a snapshot viewer; year-over-year change highlighting is a later version.
Note: the acreage figure above is the official NASS/CDFA county total, not a sum of the polygons on screen. Mapped field footprints and the official survey are produced by different methods and differ by a few percent.
