How to use

Ask any question about Napa County policy, land use, housing, water, or economic development. The agent searches the NVF archive and the live web before responding — and cites its sources. Try a starter question on the left, or type your own.

Ask NapaServe

This agent draws on three years of original local reporting, regional economic data and community polling to answer questions about Napa Valley. Ask about land use, the wine industry, housing, workforce, local government or anything shaping the valley's future.
